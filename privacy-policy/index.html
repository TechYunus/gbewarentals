Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11 June 2024

Gbewa Rentals ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Gbewa Rentals mobile application ("App"), which provides users the ability to browse through listings of apartments, rooms, cars, cameras, or anything rentable. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information Provided by Agents:

  • Full Name: Required for identity validation.
  • Phone Number: Used for communication purposes.
  • Email Address: Used for communication and account management.
  • Location Address: Required for identity validation.
  • Government Issued Identification Documents: Required to validate agent identity.
  • Passport Photo: Captured using the App camera for identity validation.

Information Provided by Users/Tenants:

  • Full Name: Required during the booking process.
  • Email or Phone Number: Required during the booking process.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To facilitate the booking and rental processes.
  • To communicate with users and agents regarding bookings, inquiries, and customer support.
  • To verify the identity of agents and ensure the security of transactions.
  • To improve our services and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except as described below:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers that perform services on our behalf, such as payment processing, data storage, and customer support.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All sensitive data (such as identification documents) is stored securely in our databases and is accessible only to authorized personnel.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us.

6. Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app. Please follow these steps:

  • Open the Gbewa Rentals app.
  • Navigate to the User Settings screen.
  • Click on the "Delete Account" button.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your account deletion.

If you encounter any issues or have questions about this process, please contact our support team at gbewarentals@gmail.com.
